## Trainer Walkthrough Script

### Setup

Show the trainer the `/trainer` dashboard and explain:

> This is a seeded prototype. It is not trying to tell you what to do. We want to learn whether it gives you useful context quickly while preserving your professional judgment.

### Tasks

1. Look at the support queue.
2. Tell us which client you would review first.
3. Explain what made you choose that client.
4. Openly react to the scenario, profile context, and session prep sections.
5. Tell us what you would do next, if anything.

### Questions

- Can you quickly tell who needs attention?
- Do the support reasons feel useful, obvious, noisy, or presumptive?
- Does the wording preserve your agency as the trainer?
- Is the profile context enough to understand the client?
- Is the session prep useful before a real session?
- What information is missing?
- What would you ignore?
- What would you change before using this with real clients?

### Specific Things To Watch

- Does the trainer understand the queue without explanation?
- Do they naturally move from support item to profile context to session prep?
- Do they object to any wording?
- Do they feel the app is making assumptions?
- Do they mention safety, scope, or referral concerns?
- Do they notice non-scale progress?
